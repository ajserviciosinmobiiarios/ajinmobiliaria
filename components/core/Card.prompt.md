Squared surface container. No rounded corners; relies on hairline borders or soft warm shadows.

```jsx
<Card tone="light" elevation="card">…</Card>
<Card tone="dark" topRule>…</Card>
```

Tones: `light`, `paper`, `cream`, `dark`. Elevations: `flat` (hairline), `card` (soft shadow), `lift` (hover/modal). `topRule` adds a 2px gold accent edge.
